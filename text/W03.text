Here’s a simple list-style overview of Monaco:

Population: About 39,000 people (one of the smallest countries in the world)

Location: On the Mediterranean coast in France (French Riviera)

Size: Around 2.1 square kilometers (second smallest country after Vatican City)

Capital: Monaco (it’s a city-state, so the country and capital are the same)

Known for:

Luxury lifestyle and wealth

Casinos, especially Monte Carlo Casino

The Monaco Grand Prix (famous Formula 1 race)

Yachts and high-end tourism

Government: Constitutional monarchy ruled by Prince Albert II

Economy:

No income tax for residents

Relies on tourism, banking, and luxury services

Language: French (official language)

Currency: Euro (€)

Fun facts:

One of the most densely populated countries in the world

Very safe with a high police presence

Surrounded almost entirely by France, with a coastline on the Mediterranean Sea
